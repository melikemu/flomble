Privacy Policy

Flomble  ·  Last updated: June 9, 2026  ·  Melike Mühür

Flomble is a color memory game. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data We Do Not Collect

Flomble does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not connect to the internet, and does not share any data with third parties.

Data Stored on Your Device

Flomble saves two pieces of information locally on your device using your browser's localStorage:

Level progress — which levels you have completed and your star ratings. This is stored under the key cf-prog.

Sound preference — whether you have muted the game. This is stored under the key cf-muted.

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your app data in your device settings.

Permissions

Flomble does not request any device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other device feature.

Children

Flomble does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date.
